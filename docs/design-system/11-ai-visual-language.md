# 11 · AI Visual Language — עמית

LawME is AI-native, and the AI has one name (עמית — "colleague"), one material (gold light),
and one temperament (a brilliant, discreet clerk). The visual language makes AI content
**instantly recognizable and never deceptive** — a lawyer must always know, at a glance,
what a human wrote and what the machine prepared.

## The one rule

**Everything AI-generated lives inside the gold mark. Nothing else wears gold.**

This bidirectional contract is the entire system: gold ⇔ AI/significance. It's why gold
discipline elsewhere (02-color) matters — dilute the gold, and you dilute the trust.

## The AIBlock — one way AI appears, everywhere

Every piece of ambient AI content (living summary, suggested task, drafted time entry,
document intelligence) renders in the same container:

```
▏ (gold-500 start-edge hairline, 2px)
▏  gold-100 wash background, radius-md, 20px padding
▏  content in normal ink typography (never gold text for content)
▏  footer: ✦ עמית · timestamp · [רענון] [👍 👎] — text-caption, foreground-faint
```

- The start-edge gold hairline (right side in RTL) is the signature — like a margin note
  from a trusted clerk.
- AI content typography is identical to human content (same fonts, sizes, ink) — AI is not
  "styled smaller"; it is *marked*, not *diminished*.
- Citations: inline chips (`radius-pill`, paper-200) naming the source document; clicking
  opens the source. Uncited AI claims about case facts are a product bug.

## States of עמית

| State | Visual |
|---|---|
| Idle | Nothing. עמית is invisible until relevant |
| Thinking | The gold breath: the block's hairline slowly drifts opacity 0.35→0.6, 2.4s cycle. No spinners, no bouncing dots |
| Streaming | Text arrives in paragraph-level fades (`motion-quick`), never per-character typewriter |
| Proposal | AI never acts silently: mutations render as a proposal card — content + [אישור] (primary) [עריכה] [דחייה] (quiet). The confirm action is ink, not gold — *the human decision is ink* |
| Error/unsure | עמית says so in plain Hebrew («לא מצאתי מקור לזה») inside the same block — uncertainty is styled identically, never hidden |

## The panel and the sparkle problem

- The עמית panel is the app's primary glass surface (05), sliding from the start edge.
  Its composer focus ring is gold; user messages sit on paper, עמית messages carry the
  gold-hairline mark.
- Iconography: עמית's glyph is a small four-point star (✦) drawn in our 1.5px line style,
  gold-600. **Banned:** purple gradients, robot faces, magic wands, pulsing rainbow borders,
  "✨ Generated by AI ✨" theater.
- In the command bar, the "שאל את עמית" group is separated by a gold hairline divider —
  the only gold inside the command bar.

## Trust choreography

- AI never modifies the workspace without a visible proposal→confirm moment (motion:
  the proposal card settles into place as real content on confirm — the transition itself
  communicates "this is now yours").
- Regeneration replaces content with a cross-fade, keeping layout stable — no jumping.
- Every AIBlock is auditable: the footer timestamp opens provenance (model, sources, when).
